THE PRESBYTERIAN

If you treat whiskey like a religion, a classic Presbyterian is just the cocktail for you.

The Presbyterian is a refreshing way to enjoy your whiskey in a casual highball drink. It is a classic cocktail that has just three common ingredients, so it is very easy to mix up.

Ingredients

  • 45ml Bourbon
  • 45ml Soda Water
  • Ginger Ale to top
  • Optional: Lemon Wedge to garnish

Directions

  1. Add the Bourbon into a highball glass over ice.
  2. Top with Ginger Ale and Soda Water.
  3. Garnish with Lemon Wedge
