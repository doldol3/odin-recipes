THE BLACKBERRY MARTINI

This Blackberry Martini is gorgeous and so refreshing!

One of the most exquisite exploitations of the blackberry is accomplished by teaming it with a little booze and presenting it in a sugar-rimmed martini glass, making what might be called a blacktini.

Ingredients

  • 40ml Gin
  • 40ml Lillet Blanc (A French still wine, brandy, herbs)
  • 10ml Crème de Mûre
  • ½ tsp Absinthe (optional)
  • Lemon twist, or Rosemary sprig, to garnish

Directions

  1. Add all your ingredients to a mixing glass with ice and stir to chill.
  2. Strain into a martini or coupe glass.
  3. Garnish with Lemon twist and Rosemary spring.
