OLD CUBAN RECIPE

This classic cocktail has over 100 years of history and a refreshing, nuanced flavor.

The cocktail takes inspiration from the Mojito, but it features a few important tweaks that result in a unique flavor experience. For starters, Saunders employs aged rum, rather than the Mojitoâ€™s traditional call for white rum. From there, simple syrup, fresh lime juice and mint continue the Mojito trajectory, but those ingredients benefit from aromatic bitters and a topper of champagne, which brings a festive dose of effervescence.

Ingredients

  • 45ml Rum
  • 60ml Prosecco
  • 25ml Gomme Syrup
  • 20ml Lime Juice
  • 2 dashes Bitters
  • 6 fresh mint leaves

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients except prosecco in a cocktail shaker with ice and shake for 20-30 seconds.
  2. Strain and pour into a coupe glass and top with prosecco.
  3. Garnish with a fresh mint leaf and serve.
